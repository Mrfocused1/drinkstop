Boy Better Know is North London history — the grime collective and label built from the ground up by brothers Skepta and JME in 2005, alongside Jammer, Frisco, Shorty and DJ Maximum. No major label, no permission asked: tunes pressed independently and t-shirts printed by hand and sold at raves, on road, wherever the scene was.

From winning Red Bull Culture Clash in 2012 to taking over The O2 for a full-day festival in 2017, BBK turned independence into an institution. That's the energy this pouch carries.

For one event only, DRINK STOP froze that legacy: three colours, real rum, the BBK stamp on the bag. In the stands, in the pit, in the function — pouch in hand.
